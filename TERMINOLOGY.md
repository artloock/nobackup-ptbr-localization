# Terminology – No Backup PT-BR Localization

This glossary documents key terminology decisions used during the PT-BR localization of **No Backup**.

Its purpose is to maintain consistency across UI, mission text, dialogue, items, warnings, and system messages.

## Neutralization Strategy

Some English structures were intentionally neutralized in PT-BR due to gameplay ambiguity between solo and co-op.

Examples included:

- removing explicit "you" when possible
- replacing plural-only commands with neutral formulations
- using operational phrasing that works for both one player and a team

Examples:
- "you first need to..." → "primeiro, é preciso..."
- "we don't know what's waiting for you down there" → "a gente não sabe o que esperar lá embaixo"
